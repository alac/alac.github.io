About Me

I'm currently a software engineer at Pocket Gems in San Fransisco, where I've been since 2012. Initially, I worked on the top 25 grossing Tap Paradise Cove, implementing features and mentoring junior devs.

In mid-2013, I became the lead iOS developer of Episode: Choose Your Story. I singlehandedly designed and maintained the application until it's launch in January 2014. I've since stepped down as lead in order to concentrate more on scaling and risk mitigation.

Before Pocket Gems, I studied Computer Science and Applied Math at U.C. Berkeley where I graduated in 2011.

In my spare time, I perform basic physiological processes, read non-fiction and make things look nicer.

I also take notes on things that I don't want to forget and post them here.
